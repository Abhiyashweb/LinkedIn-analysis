Features
Profile Strength Evaluation: Assess the overall strength of LinkedIn profiles based on various parameters.
Keyword Optimization: Analyze and suggest keywords to improve profile searchability and relevance.
Sentiment Analysis: Perform sentiment analysis on profile summaries to gauge tone and professionalism.
Connection Growth Tracking: Monitor and visualize connection growth over time.
Endorsement Recommendations: Provide personalized recommendations for skills to endorse and receive endorsements.
Content Strategy Suggestions: Offer insights and tips for effective content sharing and engagement on LinkedIn.
